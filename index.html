As a Criminal Justice student and Pre-Apprentice at Dev/Mission, I’m passionate about using data, empathy, and community engagement to create positive change within the justice system. My goal is to become a Probation Officer who not only enforces accountability but also promotes rehabilitation, education, and opportunity for youth and underserved populations.

Through my community involvement, I’ve gained a deeper understanding of the Latinx culture and the diverse needs of individuals seeking support and second chances. These experiences have strengthened my skills in public speaking, collaboration, and time management, as well as my ability to communicate effectively in both English and Spanish.

At Dev/Mission, I’m developing technical and data analysis skills that I plan to apply in the justice field — using data to identify trends, support prevention programs, and improve rehabilitation outcomes. Helping others in my native language and advocating for equitable systems are at the heart of my work.

I’m eager to continue learning, growing, and building a career that bridges technology, data, and justice reform to empower communities and inspire change.
